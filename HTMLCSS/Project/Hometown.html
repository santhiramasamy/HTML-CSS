Top three activities to do at Chennai

Sweet escape spot for you

Beaches in Chennai along the Bay of Bengal coast, it happens to be the longest beach in India and one of the longest in the world.

Those of you who love literature

Valluvar Kottam is an intricately designed chariot built in the honour of Tamil poet and saint, Thiruvalluvar. The magnificence of Valluvar Kottam can be best experienced early in the morning .

Visit National Park to feel little adventurous

Guindy National Park is a forest Home to over 300 kinds of trees and plants, 150 bird species and 15 species of mammals, reptiles and amphibians.
